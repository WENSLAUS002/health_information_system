Welcome to the careers page of Our Hospital! We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace. Explore our open vacancies, application timelines, internships, and volunteering opportunities.

Open Vacancies

We are currently seeking passionate individuals for the following positions:

  • Registered Nurse
  • Medical Assistant
  • Clinical Research Coordinator
  • Radiologic Technologist
  • Pharmacist
  • Administrative Assistant

Application Timeline

Our application process typically follows these steps:

  1. Application Submission: Submit your application through our online portal.
  2. Initial Review: Applications are reviewed by our HR team.
  3. Interviews: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for interviews.
  4. Offer: Successful candidates will receive an offer letter.
  5. Onboarding: New hires will undergo orientation and training.

Internships

We offer internship programs for students and recent graduates who wish to gain practical experience in a healthcare setting. Our internships typically last for 3-6 months and cover various departments, including:

  • Clinical Internships
  • Administrative Internships
  • Research Internships
  • Public Health Internships

To apply, please submit your resume and cover letter via our online application portal.

Volunteering

Volunteers play a vital role in supporting our hospital and enhancing the patient experience. We welcome volunteers in various capacities, including:

  • Patient Support
  • Administrative Assistance
  • Event Coordination
  • Community Outreach

If you're interested in volunteering, please fill out our volunteer application form available on our website.
