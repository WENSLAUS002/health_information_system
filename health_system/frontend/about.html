About Us

The Center for Epidemiological Modelling and Analysis (CEMA) Hospital is a premier healthcare institution committed to excellence in patient care, medical research, and community service. Founded on the principles of compassion, innovation, and collaboration, CEMA Hospital integrates cutting-edge medical technology with a team of dedicated professionals to deliver world-class healthcare solutions to the communities we serve.

At CEMA, we believe that healthcare is not just about treating illnesses; it's about improving lives. Through our focus on epidemiology, clinical care, and education, we are dedicated to shaping a healthier future for generations to come.

Our Culture

At CEMA Hospital, we nurture a culture of respect, integrity, and excellence. We believe in continuous learning, professional growth, and fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where every team member contributes to transforming lives through healthcare.

Our Departments

  • Emergency Department: Immediate life-saving care for critical medical situations.
  • Cardiology: Comprehensive cardiac diagnostics, intervention, and rehabilitation services.
  • Pediatrics: Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents.
  • Orthopedics: Treatment of musculoskeletal injuries and disorders.

  • Radiology: Advanced imaging for precise diagnostics and interventional procedures.
